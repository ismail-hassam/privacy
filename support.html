Frequently Asked Questions

How does Maidai analyze my skin?
Maidai uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your facial photos and detect various skin conditions like acne, redness, and texture issues. The analysis is performed completely on your device for maximum privacy.
Is my face data stored or shared?
No, absolutely not. Your facial data and personal information are never shared with third parties. All analysis happens locally on your device, and we prioritize your privacy above all else.
How often should I scan my face?
For best results, we recommend scanning your face 2-3 times per week at the same time of day, preferably in natural lighting. This helps track your progress accurately over time.
Can Maidai replace professional dermatologist advice?
Maidai is designed to complement your skincare routine and provide educational insights, but it should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a dermatologist for serious skin concerns.
What devices are supported?
Maidai is currently available for iOS devices. We recommend using devices with good front-facing cameras for optimal scanning results.
How do I get the best scanning results?
For optimal results: use natural lighting, keep your face clean and makeup-free, hold the phone steady, and ensure your entire face is visible in the frame.

Troubleshooting

The app won't scan my face properly
Try these steps: 1) Ensure good lighting conditions, 2) Clean your device's front camera, 3) Remove any makeup or face coverings, 4) Hold the device steady, 5) Restart the app if issues persist.
My progress tracking seems inaccurate
For consistent tracking: scan at the same time of day, use similar lighting conditions, maintain the same distance from the camera, and ensure your face is positioned similarly in each scan.
The app is running slowly
Try closing other apps running in the background, restart your device, and ensure you have the latest version of Maidai installed from the App Store.
