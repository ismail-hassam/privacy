MAIDAI Privacy Policy

Overview

MAIDAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our skin analysis application.

Information We Collect

Photos and Images

  • We temporarily access your camera or photo library when you choose to analyze your skin.
  • Images you select for analysis are processed securely.
  • Images you choose to save are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not permanently store your images on our servers.

Skin Analysis Data

  • Information about your skin conditions is processed for analysis purposes only.
  • Analysis results are stored locally on your device if you choose to save them.
  • This data is not linked to your identity.

User Input

  • Information you provide during setup (ethnicity, age, skin goals) is stored locally on your device.
  • Personal notes you add are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not collect or transmit this information to our servers.

Data Processing and Third-Party Services

Anthropic Claude API

  • When you request a skin analysis, your selected image is securely transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API.
  • The image is sent solely for the purpose of analyzing skin conditions.
  • According to Anthropic's data policies, they do not train on or permanently store customer-submitted data processed through their API.
  • The response from the API is delivered back to your device and may be stored locally if you choose to save it.
  • We do not maintain any server-side storage of your images.

Data Security

  • All communication with external APIs uses industry-standard encryption.
  • Your locally stored data is protected by your device's built-in security features.
  • We use Apple's Keychain for secure storage of API credentials.

Data Retention

  • Data is stored only on your local device.
  • You can delete all app data at any time by using the "Reset App" function in Settings.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data.

Children's Privacy

MAIDAI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
